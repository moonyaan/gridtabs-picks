# AI Made This: Type a Sentence, Get Music, a World, a Film

The new creative pipeline is just a text box. Describe a mood — get full music stems. Name a setting — get a fantasy world with maps and lore. These 8 tools turn a sentence into something you couldn't have made alone.

# 输入一句话，造出一首歌、一个世界、一部剧本

创作的门槛消失了。描述一种情绪，AI 乐手团队替你写出完整音轨。说出一个场景，魔法系统和世界地图自动成形。这 8 个工具改了一件事：你负责想象，机器负责造出来。

## Sites

### Orchestria
- **URL**: https://orchestria.tech
- **EN**: Describe a mood in plain English and a team of AI musicians — drummer, bassist, melody writer — collaborates to produce full multi-track stems you can export and use commercially. Zero music theory. Zero DAW. Export individual stems and use them commercially.
- **ZH**: 用几个词描述你想要的情绪和风格，AI 鼓手、贝斯手、旋律创作者协同写出完整的分轨音乐。每轨单独可导出、可商用。不需要 DAW，不需要乐理知识。

### Elentaria
- **URL**: https://elentaria.ai
- **EN**: Type a setting and get a complete fantasy world back — maps, history, magic systems, factions, lore. Built for novelists, game designers, and TRPG players who want a world that holds together, not just vibes.
- **ZH**: 打几个字，拿回一个完整的奇幻世界——地图、历史、魔法体系、派系、传说一应俱全。专为小说作者、游戏设计师、TRPG 玩家打造，不是碎片灵感，而是能自圆其说的完整世界观。

### Perchance
- **URL**: https://perchance.org
- **EN**: Hundreds of free, unlimited AI generators — images, stories, characters, names — built and shared by a community. No account, no tracking, no limits. The most privacy-respecting creative sandbox on the web.
- **ZH**: 数百个免费无限量的 AI 生成器——图像、故事、角色、名字——由社区构建和分享。无需注册，不追踪数据，无使用上限。互联网上最注重隐私的创意沙盒。

### Ideogram
- **URL**: https://ideogram.ai
- **EN**: AI image generation where the text in the image actually reads correctly. No garbled letters, no scrambled signs. If your concept needs words — a poster, a logo mock, a social card — this is the one that gets it right.
- **ZH**: AI 生图终于解决了文字乱码问题。图像里的字母、标题、标语，Ideogram 能准确渲染出来。做海报、社媒配图、品牌概念图——需要带文字的设计，选这个。

### VoiceDraw
- **URL**: https://voicedraw.com
- **EN**: Speak your idea out loud and watch the AI draw the diagram in real time — flowcharts, architecture maps, system designs. Faster than any whiteboard, and you never have to touch a mouse.
- **ZH**: 说出你的想法，AI 实时把它画成流程图、架构图、系统设计图。比任何白板都快，全程不需要碰鼠标。头脑风暴从来没有这么顺畅过。

### Fish Audio S2
- **URL**: https://fish.audio/s2/
- **EN**: Describe how someone should sound — nervous, laughing, whispering — and get a voice back in 150ms. Open-source, 80+ languages, expressive beyond any TTS you've heard. The model weights are yours to run.
- **ZH**: 用自然语言描述你想要的声音——紧张、大笑、低语——150ms 内拿到结果。开源、80+ 种语言、表现力超出你对 TTS 的所有预期。模型权重完全开放，可以自己跑。

### Ensemble
- **URL**: https://ensemblewriter.com
- **EN**: Professional screenplay software with AI shot-list generation built in. Write in industry-standard format, collaborate in real time, then ask the AI to generate your shot list. The first tool that treats indie filmmakers like pros, not hobbyists.
- **ZH**: 专业剧本写作工具，内置 AI 镜头分析功能。用好莱坞行业标准格式写作，实时多人协作，AI 自动生成分镜列表。第一个真正把独立电影人当专业人士对待的工具。

### Wario Synthesis Engine
- **URL**: https://wario.style
- **EN**: Upload any MIDI file and get it back as Game Boy chiptune, rendered entirely in the browser with the Web Audio API. Pure play — the creator built it "just for lols." But the results genuinely slap.
- **ZH**: 上传任意 MIDI 文件，浏览器里直接返回 Game Boy 8-bit 芯片音乐版本。作者说这个项目"just for lols"，但出来的效果真的挺好的。把你最喜欢的曲子送回 1989 年。
