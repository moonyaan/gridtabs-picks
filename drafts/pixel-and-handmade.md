# Pixel, Ink, and Handmade Websites

8 websites that still feel handmade. Pixel-art drum machines, hand-drawn animations, dot-matrix life calendars — rough edges, warm textures, real craft.

# 像素、墨迹和手工造物

当 AI 可以生成一切的时候，这些网站还是手工做的。粗糙的边缘，温暖的质感，打开就知道有人在认真做这件事。

## Sites

### Beats
- **URL**: https://beats.lasagna.pizza
- **EN**: A pixel-art drum machine in your browser — monochrome interface, VT323 font, lo-fi warmth no AI could fake.
- **ZH**: 像素风鼓机，打开浏览器就能敲节拍。单色界面加 VT323 字体，那种 lo-fi 的温度是 AI 生成不出来的。

### StoryMotion
- **URL**: https://storymotion.video
- **EN**: Turns technical diagrams into wobbly, hand-drawn animations — like someone explaining things on a whiteboard.
- **ZH**: 把技术图表变成手绘动画。歪歪扭扭的线条让复杂概念变得亲切，像有人在白板上边画边讲。

### Inklings
- **URL**: https://inklings.social
- **EN**: Kids draw, parents write, and every month it gets printed into a real book for your shelf.
- **ZH**: 孩子画画，家长写字，每个月印成一本实体书。在无限刷新的时代，这个社交网络最终会出现在你的书架上。

### Its Hover
- **URL**: https://www.itshover.com
- **EN**: An icon library where everything moves — every hover, every click has its own animation. Someone sat there and made each one by hand.
- **ZH**: 这个图标库里每个图标都会动。悬停、点击、加载，每个动作都有自己的动画。你能感觉到有人一个一个做出来的。

### Calmly Writer
- **URL**: https://www.calmlywriter.com
- **EN**: Focus mode dims everything except the current paragraph — a typewriter for the browser age.
- **ZH**: 一个只剩下你和文字的写作工具。专注模式下只有当前段落是亮的，像一台只显示当前行的打字机。

### Remainders
- **URL**: https://remainders.vercel.app
- **EN**: Your life as 4,160 dots, one per week. Dot-matrix wallpaper that makes time feel real every time you unlock.
- **ZH**: 把你的人生变成 4160 个点，每个点是一周。点阵风格，不花哨但看一眼就忘不掉。每次解锁手机，时间都在回望你。

### Colorify Rocks
- **URL**: https://colorify.rocks
- **EN**: Type a mood, get a palette. "Sunday morning" becomes amber and cream — simple idea, surprisingly fun to play with.
- **ZH**: 输入一种感觉，得到一组颜色。把抽象的文字变成具体的色彩，像一次小小的通感体验。

### Radio Garden
- **URL**: https://radio.garden
- **EN**: Spin a 3D globe, land on any radio station on Earth. No algorithm, no recommendations — just you and a planet of sound.
- **ZH**: 转动 3D 地球仪，落在地球上任何一个电台。没有算法，没有推荐引擎，只有你和一个充满声音的星球。
