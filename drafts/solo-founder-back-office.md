# Solo Founder Back Office: 9 Tools to Run It All Yourself

Nine tools covering CRM, billing, meetings, and changelogs — built for the solo founder who wears every hat but still wants to run a tight operation.

# 一个人撑起一家公司的工具箱

没有 HR，没有财务，没有专职市场，但公司还是得转起来。这九个工具是专门为一个人承担所有职能的创始人设计的——会议、客户、计费、更新日志，每一块都有专门的解法，而不是凑合。

## Sites

### Granola
- **URL**: https://www.granola.ai
- **EN**: Solo founders live in back-to-back calls. Granola captures audio directly from your Mac and turns your rough mid-meeting notes into structured documents — no bot joining the call, no transcript to clean up afterward. When every meeting is also a sales call, a product review, and an investor update, you can't afford to miss what was said.
- **ZH**: 一个人开会的时候还要边记笔记，边想下一步怎么说。Granola 直接从电脑音频里转录，再把你写下的碎片整理成结构化文档。会后不需要整理，不需要机器人在会议里亮相，你的注意力可以完整地放在对话上。

### Attio
- **URL**: https://attio.com
- **EN**: CRM that doesn't assume you have a sales team. Attio auto-syncs your emails and calls, lets you define your own data model — clients, investors, partners, whatever your GTM actually looks like — and has an AI you can ask questions in plain language. For a solo founder, it's the difference between knowing your pipeline and guessing.
- **ZH**: 大多数 CRM 是为销售团队设计的，Attio 是少数真正适合创始人一个人用的。数据结构完全自定义，邮件和通话自动同步，还能直接用自然语言问 AI 当前的客户情况。对单人公司来说，知道自己的管道状态和凭感觉猜，结果是不一样的。

### Lindy
- **URL**: https://www.lindy.ai
- **EN**: An AI assistant that handles the repetitive part of the job: routing inboxes, scheduling, following up on leads, sending onboarding emails, logging calls. The solo founder's dirty secret is that half the day disappears into these things. Lindy runs them 24/7 while you work on the things only you can do.
- **ZH**: 一个人公司里有一半时间会消失在重复性事务里——回邮件、排日程、跟进客户、发入职邮件。Lindy 是专门接管这些的 AI 助理，24 小时运行，让你的精力可以集中在只有你能做的决策上。

### ChaChing
- **URL**: https://chaching.io
- **EN**: Billing platform that costs about half what Stripe Billing charges for the same features. Subscriptions, invoices, usage-based pricing — built for SaaS, priced for a company without a CFO. The money you keep at the billing layer is money you didn't have to raise.
- **ZH**: 订阅计费的费率比 Stripe Billing 低一半，功能一样覆盖：订阅管理、发票、用量计费。没有 CFO 的公司，在计费这层能省下来的钱，就是少融的一轮钱。

### VaultLeap
- **URL**: https://vaultleap.com
- **EN**: If your company is outside the US but your customers are inside it, getting paid is a structural problem. VaultLeap gives you a real USD or EUR account — ACH, SEPA, SPEI, BPIX — without requiring a US entity. Incoming payments convert to USDC/EURC automatically. The Visa card works everywhere. For a global solo founder, this is banking infrastructure that actually fits.
- **ZH**: 如果你在美国以外创业但客户在美国，收款本身就是一个系统性问题。VaultLeap 提供真实的美元/欧元账户，不需要美国公司实体，收到的钱自动转为稳定币保值，附带 Visa 借记卡。对全球远程创始人来说，这是第一个真正符合现实情况的收款方案。

### Tycoon AI
- **URL**: https://tycoon.us
- **EN**: If Lindy handles the repetitive tasks, Tycoon hands you an entire AI team for the strategic ones. Natural language to launch a marketing campaign, spin up customer support flows, or run competitive analysis. It won't replace judgment, but it does replace the six roles you can't afford to hire.
- **ZH**: Lindy 处理重复性事务，Tycoon 给你一个用自然语言驱动的 AI 团队来处理更复杂的职能——市场推广、客户支持、竞品分析。它替代不了你的判断，但能替代那六个你雇不起的岗位。

### Notra
- **URL**: https://www.usenotra.com
- **EN**: Content marketing is the solo founder's most ignored back-office function. Notra connects to GitHub, Linear, and Slack, reads what shipped, and generates changelog entries and blog draft posts in your brand's voice. The update that would've taken an afternoon to write happens automatically every time you merge a PR.
- **ZH**: 内容营销是单人公司最常被忽略的后台职能。Notra 接入 GitHub、Linear 和 Slack，看到你合并了 PR，自动生成符合你品牌语气的更新日志和博客草稿。原来要花一下午写的更新内容，现在在你发代码的同时就出来了。

### Slideshot
- **URL**: https://slideshot.ai
- **EN**: Product demos are the currency of early-stage selling. Slideshot uses an AI agent to record your product automatically — you describe the flow, it clicks through and produces a polished video. No more spending a Sunday re-recording because the product changed. Demos stay current without effort.
- **ZH**: 早期销售靠的就是产品演示视频。Slideshot 用 AI 代理自动录制——你描述操作流程，它自动点击录制，生成专业演示视频。产品更新后不需要重新花一个周末重录，演示随时保持最新。

### Devtimate
- **URL**: https://devtimate.com
- **EN**: Solo technical founders still have to quote projects, scope work, and respond to client requirements fast. Devtimate takes a requirement document and produces a scoped estimate with hours, roles, and tasks — in the time it used to take just to read the spec. The quote that goes out the same day is the quote that often wins.
- **ZH**: 即便是独立技术型创始人，也需要给项目报价、拆分工作范围、快速回应客户需求。Devtimate 上传需求文档，自动生成带工时、角色、任务拆分的估算表。以前读完需求文档才开始估算，现在传进去就出来了。当天回的报价，通常更容易拿到项目。
