# 8 Websites Worth Opening This Weekend

From internet history to ambient noise to AI builders — 8 sites that actually pulled me in this week. Most need no signup.

# 周末值得打开的 8 个网站

互联网博物馆、白噪音生成器、AI 建站工具——8 个我这周打开后真的多看了几眼的网站。

## Sites

### Neal.fun
- **URL**: https://neal.fun/internet-artifacts
- **EN**: Scroll through 57 internet milestones, from the first email to early YouTube. It's interactive history that doesn't feel like homework.
- **ZH**: 互联网历史互动时间线，从第一封邮件到早期 YouTube 一共 57 个节点。不是枯燥的编年史，每个点进去都有意思。

### Moodist
- **URL**: https://moodist.mvze.net
- **EN**: Mix 84 ambient sounds however you want. Rain + distant train + soft static. I use it when I need to focus or just sit still for a minute.
- **ZH**: 84 种环境音随便叠加。窗外雨声 + 远处火车 + 轻微白噪音。我用它来专注，或者单纯发个呆。

### GummySearch
- **URL**: https://gummysearch.com
- **EN**: Sorts 130k+ subreddits by pain points, feature requests, and money talk. If you're building something, this is where you find what people actually want.
- **ZH**: 把 Reddit 13 万个子版块按痛点、需求、花钱讨论自动分类。做产品的人进去基本出不来。

### Godly
- **URL**: https://godly.website
- **EN**: The most visually striking websites on the internet, updated daily. Pure inspiration if you're tired of template designs.
- **ZH**: 互联网上视觉最炸的网页设计合集，每天更新。看腻了模板网站的话，这里全是灵感。

### SEESAW
- **URL**: https://seesaw.website
- **EN**: Unlike Godly's "wow" factor, this one collects sites that just feel good to use. Subtle animations, clean layouts, warmth over flashiness.
- **ZH**: 跟 Godly 的「炸眼」不同，这里收集的是「用起来舒服」的网站。细节动画、克制排版、温度感大于炫技。

### Lovable
- **URL**: https://lovable.dev
- **EN**: Describe an app in plain English, get a working full-stack project in minutes. Best way to start something on a Saturday morning.
- **ZH**: 用自然语言描述一个应用，几分钟给你一个能跑的全栈项目。周六早上想做点什么的时候，从这开始最快。

### bolt.new
- **URL**: https://bolt.new
- **EN**: Build, run, and deploy a web app entirely in your browser. No terminal, no local setup. Just type what you want and watch it build.
- **ZH**: 在浏览器里直接构建、运行、部署 Web 应用。不用终端，不用本地环境，说清楚你要什么，看着它给你搭出来。

### Griply
- **URL**: https://griply.app
- **EN**: Goals, habits, and tasks in one clean space. A good weekend is also when you figure out what actually matters in your week.
- **ZH**: 目标、习惯、任务放在一个干净的界面里。好的周末，也是想清楚「这周什么最重要」的时候。
