# Visual Design Tools for Makers

Screenshots, slides, product demos — these tools handle the last mile of visual presentation, so your work actually looks as good as it is.

# 让作品看起来和它本身一样好

截图、幻灯片、产品演示——这些工具专门负责"视觉收尾"，让你的成果看起来和它实际上一样好。

## Sites

### Screen Studio
- **URL**: https://screen.studio
- **EN**: Screen recordings are usually forgettable. Screen Studio makes them something people actually watch — auto-zoom follows your cursor, the output looks polished without any post-editing work.
- **ZH**: 大多数录屏都平淡无奇。Screen Studio 不一样——自动缩放跟随光标，录出来的东西直接能发，不用再剪辑润色。

### Photoroom
- **URL**: https://www.photoroom.com
- **EN**: Product photos with cluttered backgrounds kill conversions. Photoroom removes the background, drops in a clean scene, and lets you batch-process a hundred images at once — the kind of thing that used to require a studio.
- **ZH**: 乱背景毁产品图。Photoroom 一键抠图、AI 换场景、批量处理百张图——以前要租摄影棚才能搞定的事，现在几分钟出成品。

### Pika
- **URL**: https://pika.style
- **EN**: Your screenshot is fine. Pika makes it beautiful — add a browser frame, a gradient background, drop shadow, then resize it for Product Hunt, Twitter, or the App Store in one click.
- **ZH**: 截图能用，但 Pika 能让它好看——加浏览器框、换渐变背景、加阴影，一键输出 Product Hunt / Twitter / App Store 规格，省去排版时间。

### Kodo
- **URL**: https://www.usekodo.ai
- **EN**: Describe what you want — "a poster for a product launch, dark and minimal" — and Kodo generates a layered, editable design with adjustable text, colors, and layout. A real design file, not a locked image you can't touch.
- **ZH**: 说你想要什么——"发布会用的极简深色海报"——Kodo 直接生成可编辑分层设计稿，文字颜色布局都能调，不是让你对着 AI 生成图傻眼。

### Chronicle
- **URL**: https://chroniclehq.com
- **EN**: Most AI slide tools give you bullet points in Comic Sans. Chronicle actually has taste — it builds a narrative structure first, then lays out slides that look like a designer touched them. Templates made by people from Apple and McKinsey.
- **ZH**: 大多数 AI 做的幻灯片像 PPT 模板堆砌。Chronicle 不同——先生成叙事结构，再排版设计，模板来自 Apple、McKinsey 的设计师，有真正的设计感。

### Bazaar
- **URL**: https://bazaar.it
- **EN**: SaaS demo videos are painful to make — screen recordings don't cut it, but hiring video editors is expensive. Bazaar takes your screenshots or Figma files and turns them into animated product demos with a proper timeline editor.
- **ZH**: 产品演示视频难做——录屏没有说服力，请视频编辑又贵。Bazaar 拿你的截图或 Figma 稿，直接合成动态产品演示，有完整时间轴可以编辑。

### Cardboard
- **URL**: https://usecardboard.com
- **EN**: Video editing described as "the Cursor for video" — you tell it what to do in plain language and it executes on the timeline. Silence removal, captions, color grading, search by scene content. All in the browser.
- **ZH**: 被称为"视频领域的 Cursor"——用文字说你想要什么，它直接在时间轴上执行。去静音、加字幕、调色、按画面内容搜素材，全在浏览器里完成。

### SVG Backgrounds
- **URL**: https://svgbackgrounds.com
- **EN**: 48 free, customizable SVG patterns — geometric, gradient, textured, abstract. Pick one, adjust the color, copy the CSS. The kind of library that quietly improves every landing page and social graphic you make.
- **ZH**: 48 套免费可定制 SVG 图案——几何、渐变、纹理、抽象各种风格。选好、调颜色、复制 CSS。不起眼但实用，能静悄悄地让你做的每张图和每个落地页好看一截。
