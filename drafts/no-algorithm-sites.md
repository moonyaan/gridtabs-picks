# Human Web: 8 Sites With No Algorithm, No Feed

No recommendation engine. No infinite scroll. Just things real people built, collected, or recorded — and left online for anyone to find.

# 在算法时代找回真实感

没有推荐引擎，没有无限滚动。只有真实的人类制造的东西——经典 DOS 程序、公版书籍、陌生人窗景、音乐时光机。打开有一种奇特的平静。

## Sites

### RetroTick
- **URL**: https://retrotick.com
- **EN**: Run classic DOS and Windows programs in your browser. Made by real people — studios, lone coders in basements. Decades old. Still works.
- **ZH**: 在浏览器里直接运行 DOS 和早期 Windows 程序。这些软件是真实的人写的，有时是整个团队，有时是一个人在地下室。几十年了，还能跑。

### Printable Classics
- **URL**: https://printableclassics.com
- **EN**: Public domain books, beautifully typeset and ready to print at home. Moby Dick, Dracula, Alice in Wonderland — made for people who still want to hold a book they assembled themselves.
- **ZH**: 公版经典文学，精心排版，直接在家打印装订。《白鲸》《德古拉》《爱丽丝》——给那些还想亲手装订一本书的人。

### WindowSwap
- **URL**: https://window-swap.com
- **EN**: Strangers filmed their windows and uploaded. Open one: rain in Oslo, a cat in Seoul. Nobody's curating your view.
- **ZH**: 世界各地的陌生人录下自己窗外的风景，上传到这里。你随机打开一扇——可能是奥斯陆的雨，可能是首尔路边走过的猫。没有算法在帮你选。

### Radiooooo
- **URL**: https://radiooooo.com
- **EN**: Pick a country and a decade — 1900s to now — and hear what people actually listened to there. No playlists, no curation.
- **ZH**: 在地图上点一个国家，选一个年代，从 1900 年代到现在都有。然后听听那个地方那个时候的人在听什么。没有推荐歌单，没有编辑精选，只有那时候真实存在的音乐。

### Artvee
- **URL**: https://artvee.com
- **EN**: Tens of thousands of high-resolution public domain artworks, free to use. Made by people long gone — still open to anyone.
- **ZH**: 数万件超高清公版艺术作品，免费下载可商用。画这些的人早就不在了，但作品还在，免费，谁都能用。

### How Browsers Work
- **URL**: https://howbrowserswork.com
- **EN**: One person's interactive guide to how browsers actually work — DNS, TCP, HTML parsing, rendering. The kind of deep, patient explanation that only a human who cares can write.
- **ZH**: 一个人写的交互式浏览器工作原理教程。从 DNS 到渲染管线，每一步都用动画拆解。那种只有真正在乎的人才写得出来的耐心解释。

### Concludia
- **URL**: https://concludia.org
- **EN**: A tool for mapping the logic behind any argument — visually, step by step. Good for when you want to think something through without asking an AI what to think.
- **ZH**: 把任何论证的逻辑可视化地图出来，一步步拆解前提和推论。适合那种想自己想清楚，不想让 AI 替你想的时候用。

### epilog.tv
- **URL**: https://epilog.tv
- **EN**: Rate every episode of every show you've watched, build a visual record of your actual taste — not what the algorithm guesses you'll like.
- **ZH**: 给你看过的每部剧、每一集打分，它帮你生成一份你的观剧档案。不是算法猜你喜欢什么，是你自己留下的证明。
