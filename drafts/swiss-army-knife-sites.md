# 8 Sites That Do the Work of Ten Apps

One site, dozens of tools. These eight do the work of ten apps each — and most of them are free.

# 一个网址顶十个工具

每个都是某个领域的百宝箱，打开一个网址就能解决一堆问题。大部分免费。

## Sites

### 10015 Tools
- **URL**: https://10015.io
- **EN**: Hundreds of micro-tools — text formatting, image editing, CSS generators, QR codes, color pickers — in a single tab. The kind of site that kills ten bookmarks at once.
- **ZH**: 文本处理、图片编辑、CSS 生成、二维码、颜色选择器……数百个小工具塞在一个网址里。打开一次，书签栏可以删掉十个。

### fffuel
- **URL**: https://www.fffuel.co
- **EN**: 60+ SVG generators for gradients, patterns, blobs, textures, and shapes. Every time you need a background or decorative element, this one site has you covered.
- **ZH**: 60 多个 SVG 生成器，渐变、纹理、波浪、色块、图案全都有。每次做项目需要背景素材，来这一个地方就够了。

### BentoPDF
- **URL**: https://www.bentopdf.com
- **EN**: Merge, split, compress, convert, encrypt — all in your browser. No file ever leaves your machine. The PDF toolkit you reach for instead of installing yet another app.
- **ZH**: 合并、拆分、压缩、转格式、加密——全在浏览器里完成，文件不上传到任何服务器。以后处理 PDF 不用再装桌面软件了。

### Raycast
- **URL**: https://www.raycast.com
- **EN**: On Mac, a single keyboard shortcut opens an entire productivity layer — app launcher, clipboard manager, window control, AI assistant, and 1,300+ community extensions. One tool that absorbs dozens.
- **ZH**: Mac 上按一个快捷键，打开一整层生产力工具——应用启动、剪贴板管理、窗口控制、AI 助手，外加 1300 多个社区扩展。一个工具吞掉了几十个。

### n8n
- **URL**: https://n8n.io
- **EN**: 400+ integrations, visual workflow builder, and you host it yourself. The open-source glue between every tool you use — your data never leaves your server.
- **ZH**: 400 多个集成、可视化工作流编辑器，而且你自己托管。开源的万能胶，把你用的工具全串起来，数据留在自己服务器上。

### Gumloop
- **URL**: https://www.gumloop.com
- **EN**: Where n8n is for developers, Gumloop is for everyone else. Drag, drop, connect 125+ apps, and let AI handle the logic. Your whole team can build automations without writing a line.
- **ZH**: 如果说 n8n 是给开发者的，Gumloop 就是给所有人的。拖拽连接 125 个以上应用，逻辑交给 AI。整个团队都能搭自动化，不用写一行代码。

### Excalidraw
- **URL**: https://excalidraw.com
- **EN**: Need to sketch an idea? Open Excalidraw. Flowcharts, wireframes, system diagrams — all come out in that hand-drawn style. No signup, real-time collab, works in your browser.
- **ZH**: 想画个东西说明一下？打开 Excalidraw 就行。流程图、线框图、系统架构——出来全是手绘风格。不用注册，实时协作，浏览器里直接画。

### Dify
- **URL**: https://dify.ai
- **EN**: An open-source platform for building AI agents, chatbots, and workflows — visually. Plug in any LLM, add your knowledge base, deploy. One platform, many AI products — without starting from scratch each time.
- **ZH**: 开源的 AI 应用搭建平台，拖拽式编排 Agent、聊天机器人、自动化流程。接入任意大模型，挂上知识库，直接部署。一个平台搭出聊天机器人、知识问答、自动化流程——不用每次从零开始。
