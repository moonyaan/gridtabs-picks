# 10 Apps Better When You Own the Server

Cloud services are convenient until they're not — price hikes, shutdowns, data breaches. These ten open-source apps do the same job, with your data staying exactly where you put it.

# 10 个值得自己搭的开源应用

订阅费涨价、服务关停、数据泄露——用云服务的风险你懂。这十个开源应用，功能一样，但数据只在你的服务器上。

## Sites

### Immich
- **URL**: https://immich.app
- **EN**: Google Photos does everything right — except it owns your photos. Immich is the open-source version of that experience: mobile upload, face recognition, albums, sharing. Your library, your server.
- **ZH**: Google Photos 什么都好，就是照片在它手里。Immich 是同等体验的开源版：手机自动上传、人脸识别、相册、分享。照片库还是你的。

### Paperless-ngx
- **URL**: https://paperless-ngx.com
- **EN**: Scan a document, forget about it, search for it three years later — and find it in seconds. Paperless-ngx runs OCR on everything you throw at it and turns your paper pile into a searchable archive.
- **ZH**: 扫进去，忘掉它，三年后搜一下，秒找到。Paperless-ngx 对所有文件跑 OCR，把纸堆变成永久可搜索的数字档案。

### Vikunja
- **URL**: https://vikunja.io
- **EN**: Todoist, Trello, and Asana all charge monthly. Vikunja gives you lists, kanban, Gantt, and table views — self-hosted, free forever, built in the EU. Even the mascot is a llama.
- **ZH**: Todoist、Trello、Asana 都要月付。Vikunja 给你列表、看板、甘特图、表格视图——自托管，永久免费，欧盟出品。吉祥物是只羊驼。

### Uptime Kuma
- **URL**: https://uptime.kuma.pet
- **EN**: If you're running your own services, you need to know when they go down before your users do. Uptime Kuma monitors everything and pings you on Telegram, Slack, or email the moment something breaks.
- **ZH**: 自己搭了服务，得在用户发现之前知道它挂了。Uptime Kuma 监控你的全部服务，一出问题立刻通过 Telegram、Slack 或邮件告警。

### Blinko
- **URL**: https://blinko.space
- **EN**: A flash-note app with AI search built in — ask a question in plain language, it finds the note you wrote six months ago. Everything stays on your server. No subscription.
- **ZH**: 带 AI 搜索的闪念笔记——用自然语言提问，找到你六个月前写的那条记录。数据全在自己服务器，没有订阅。

### IT-Tools
- **URL**: https://it-tools.tech
- **EN**: 86+ developer utilities in one place — encode, decode, hash, format, convert, generate. Use the hosted version or drop it on your internal network. One tab that replaces twenty bookmarks.
- **ZH**: 86 个以上开发工具集合在一处——编码解码、哈希、格式转换、生成器。可以用公开版，也可以部署到内网。一个标签页替换二十个书签。

### SimpleLogin
- **URL**: https://simplelogin.io
- **EN**: Every site you sign up for gets a different email alias. Spam? Delete that alias. Data breach? That alias was never linked to your real inbox. Owned by Proton, open source, self-hostable.
- **ZH**: 每个你注册的网站，都给它一个不同的邮箱别名。收到垃圾邮件就删掉那个别名，数据泄露也追不回到你的真实邮箱。Proton 旗下，开源，可自托管。

### Tandoor
- **URL**: https://tandoor.dev
- **EN**: A recipe manager that imports from thousands of sites, plans meals, generates shopping lists, and tracks nutrition. No ads, no paywalls, no data harvesting. Just your recipes.
- **ZH**: 能从数千个网站导入食谱、规划膳食、生成购物清单、计算营养。没有广告，没有付费墙，没有数据收集。只有你的食谱。

### Super Productivity
- **URL**: https://super-productivity.com
- **EN**: Task manager, time tracker, and Pomodoro timer in one — syncs with GitHub, GitLab, and Jira issues. 100% offline, no account required. The kind of tool that doesn't disappear when you stop paying.
- **ZH**: 任务管理 + 时间追踪 + 番茄钟三合一，同步 GitHub、GitLab、Jira 工单。100% 离线，不需要账号。不会因为停止付款就消失的那种工具。

### n8n
- **URL**: https://n8n.io
- **EN**: The self-hosted glue between all your other apps. 400+ integrations, a visual workflow builder, running entirely on your server. Automate anything without your data flowing through someone else's cloud.
- **ZH**: 把你所有应用串起来的自托管胶水。400 多个集成，可视化工作流编辑器，跑在你的服务器上。自动化任何事情，数据不经过别人的云。
