# Open a Tab, Start Playing

Nine browser games you can pick up in seconds — daily puzzles, creative toys, and weird interactive art. No installs, no accounts, just play.

# 打开就能玩

九个浏览器小世界，有每日解谜，有沙画键盘，有奇妙互动艺术。不用装任何东西，新标签页打开就开始。

## Sites

### putt.day
- **URL**: https://putt.day
- **EN**: A new mini golf hole unlocks every midnight — each one handcrafted, each one different. This is the daily-ritual kind of browser game: quick enough for a coffee break, competitive enough to keep you coming back.
- **ZH**: 每天午夜解锁一个全新球洞，设计师手工制作，拖拽瞄准挥杆，两分钟完事。这种"每日一关"的节奏，是最容易养成习惯的浏览器游戏形式。

### Gerrymandle
- **URL**: https://gerrymandle.cc
- **EN**: A daily puzzle where you redraw voting district lines to swing an election your way. It's the rare game that teaches you something real — and makes you a little uncomfortable about it.
- **ZH**: 每天一题：在六边形网格上重新划选区，让你的政党多赢几席。玩完之后你会真正理解什么叫选区操控，那种感觉有点微妙。

### QWERTYS
- **URL**: https://smartkeys.so/qwertys
- **EN**: Tetris, but the falling pieces are keyboard keys that have to land in their correct QWERTY positions. A clever mashup that feels familiar until it suddenly doesn't.
- **ZH**: 俄罗斯方块变种：从天而降的是字母键，你得把它们放到 QWERTY 键盘的正确格子里。比想象中难，也比想象中上瘾。

### Eyeball
- **URL**: https://eyeball.rory.codes
- **EN**: Click the line. That's it. The simplest possible challenge — and yet you'll keep going, trying to beat your last score by a fraction of a pixel.
- **ZH**: 点那条线。就这一件事。极简到荒谬，但你会一局一局刷下去，就为了比上次精准那么一点点。

### Patatap
- **URL**: https://patatap.com
- **EN**: Every key on your keyboard triggers a different animation and sound. Tap randomly and you're making noise; slow down and compose something almost musical. One of those browser experiences you stumble into and lose ten minutes.
- **ZH**: 每个键都有专属的动画和声音，随机敲是乱玩，慢慢敲能创作出接近音乐的东西。那种"手指停不下来"的感觉是真实的。

### This Is Sand
- **URL**: https://thisissand.com
- **EN**: Pour colored sand with your mouse, watch it settle into layers, build something that looks like geology. It's meditative in a way that's hard to explain until you've done it for ten minutes.
- **ZH**: 选颜色，用鼠标倒沙，沙粒自然堆叠出层次。说解压有点俗气，但你打开之后大概会安静地倒上一会儿才关掉。

### Koalas to the Max
- **URL**: https://koalastothemax.com
- **EN**: Hover over the screen and circles split into smaller circles, which split again, revealing a hidden image underneath. The interaction itself is the reward — smooth, satisfying, impossible to do slowly.
- **ZH**: 鼠标悬过去，大圆圈不断分裂成小圆圈，最终拼出隐藏图像。那种层层揭开的手感非常好，很难只做一次。

### Pointer Pointer
- **URL**: https://pointerpointer.com
- **EN**: Park your cursor anywhere on the screen. The site finds a photo of a real person pointing at exactly that spot. It works every time, for every position, which makes it funnier and stranger the more you test it.
- **ZH**: 把光标停在屏幕任意位置，网站会自动找出一张有人用手指指着那个位置的真实照片。每次都准，你越测越觉得离奇。

### Pixel Thoughts
- **URL**: https://pixelthoughts.co
- **EN**: Type whatever's bothering you, then watch it drift away into space over 60 seconds. Silly? Maybe. But the scale-shift — from your specific worry to the entire universe — does something that's hard to argue with.
- **ZH**: 把烦恼写进去，看着它在 60 秒里慢慢缩小消失在宇宙里。听起来有点中二，但那个"宇宙视角"的时刻确实能让人松一口气。
