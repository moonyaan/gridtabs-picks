# Always-On Internet: 8 Tools That Watch for You

No refreshing. No push notifications. These tools stay connected so you check in when ready — and actually find something worth your time.

# 永远在线的互联网

不用刷，不用等推送——这些工具主动帮你盯着世界。你随时来，它们随时有料。

## Sites

### Feedboard
- **URL**: https://feedboard.app
- **EN**: Reddit, Hacker News, Product Hunt, and CoinGecko — all in one customizable board. Instead of opening four tabs and losing your place in each one, Feedboard holds everything in a single live view you can actually scan.
- **ZH**: Reddit、HN、Product Hunt、CoinGecko 聚合进一块看板，信息流随时在滚，你决定看哪几路。

### SitDeck
- **URL**: https://sitdeck.com
- **EN**: A real-time intelligence dashboard pulling from 180+ sources — geopolitical events, natural disasters, market moves, security threats. The kind of situational awareness most people only get from scrolling 15 different feeds.
- **ZH**: 接入 180 多个数据源的实时情报仪表盘。地缘政治、自然灾害、市场波动、安全威胁，一块屏幕盯着世界。

### Daigest
- **URL**: https://daige.st
- **EN**: Set it up once: monitor YouTube channels, RSS feeds, Reddit threads, and Slack channels — then get an AI-written briefing on your schedule. You decide what matters; Daigest watches it for you and sends the digest when you want it.
- **ZH**: 把 YouTube、RSS、Reddit、Slack 设置好，AI 按你定的时间表自动整理摘要发给你。你只需要决定盯什么，剩下的交给它。

### Xikipedia
- **URL**: https://xikipedia.org
- **EN**: Wikipedia reimagined as a living feed. Swipe through articles, and a local algorithm quietly learns what you actually find interesting — no data leaves your device. It's the web's biggest encyclopedia, finally presented like something you'd actually read.
- **ZH**: 把维基百科变成可刷的信息流，推荐算法在你本机跑，数据不出设备。世界上最大的百科全书，终于有了让人想翻下去的方式。

### Rebrain.gg
- **URL**: https://rebrain.gg
- **EN**: Learning conversations, not doom-scrolling. New threads go up every day on programming, languages, and skills people are actually trying to build. Browsing it feels useful rather than compulsive.
- **ZH**: 以学习话题为核心的社区，编程、语言、技能讨论每天都有新动态。刷它的感觉是有收获，不是停不下来的那种无意义消耗。

### Hive Index
- **URL**: https://thehiveindex.com
- **EN**: 4,100+ online communities indexed by topic and platform — Discord servers, Reddit subs, Slack groups. If you're looking for where your people actually hang out, someone's already mapped it here.
- **ZH**: 收录了 4100 多个在线社区，按话题和平台分类。你想找同类圈子，这里已经把版图摸清楚了。

### 50 Hacks
- **URL**: https://50hacks.co
- **EN**: The internet votes on the 50 best productivity tips. The list keeps shifting as people submit and upvote, so there's always something new sitting at the top — and the bar for making it is set by real users, not an editorial team.
- **ZH**: 社区投票选出的 50 条最佳生产力技巧，用户持续提交和投票，榜单实时在变。有用没用，一看点赞数就知道。

### Explore.org Live Cams
- **URL**: https://explore.org/livecams
- **EN**: Live wildlife cameras streaming from Africa and beyond — lions, elephants, hippos in their actual habitat, right now. No algorithm, no highlights reel. Just whatever the animals are doing this second.
- **ZH**: 来自非洲等地的野生动物实时直播镜头。此刻狮子在做什么、河马在哪，你直接看。没有剪辑，没有算法，就是现在。
