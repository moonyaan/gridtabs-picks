# 9 Weird Corners of the Internet Worth Exploring

The internet is stranger than your feed suggests. Here are 9 sites that surprise, unsettle, or just make you stare — no algorithm needed to find them odd.

# 互联网奇物馆：9 个真正奇特的地方

算法推给你的只是互联网的一小片。这里收了 9 个真正奇特的地方——有的让你惊，有的让你静，有的让你想半天为什么会有人做这个。

## Sites

### Knock-Knock.net
- **URL**: https://knock-knock.net
- **EN**: Right now, a bot somewhere is trying to log into a random server on the internet. Knock-Knock makes this invisible constant assault visible — a live globe, country leaderboards, real usernames and passwords being attempted this second. The internet's background radiation, finally something you can watch.
- **ZH**: 此刻，某台暴露在公网的服务器正在被扫描。Knock-Knock 把这场永不停歇的入侵尝试变成了一张实时可视化的地图——攻击来源、被尝试的用户名和密码、每分钟攻击次数，全在屏幕上滚动。互联网的背景辐射，原来长这样。

### All The Views
- **URL**: https://alltheviews.world
- **EN**: Someone built a custom algorithm to compute every unobstructed sightline on Earth — 4.5 billion of them. The longest goes 530km across the Hindu Kush. This is what happens when geographic curiosity meets obsessive computation.
- **ZH**: 有人造了一个算法，把地球上所有无遮挡视线全算了一遍——共 45 亿条。最长的一条从兴都库什山脉延伸 530 公里。这是地理好奇心和计算机痴迷碰在一起会发生什么。

### LLM Holdem
- **URL**: https://llmholdem.com
- **EN**: GPT-4 and Claude sit down at a poker table. Who bluffs? Who folds? Nobody knows. Watch different AI models play Texas Hold'em against each other — turns out their decision-making styles are weirdly distinct.
- **ZH**: 把 GPT-4 和 Claude 放到一张德州扑克桌上，看谁虚张声势，谁选择弃牌。不同 AI 的决策风格出奇地不一样。这是最奇特的 AI 能力测试，也是最好看的牌局。

### Glitchy
- **URL**: https://glitchycam.com
- **EN**: Point your camera at anything. Glitchy circuit-bends it in real time — the kind of distortion that used to require a soldering iron and a broken VCR. Everything happens locally, nothing leaves your device.
- **ZH**: 把摄像头对准任何东西，Glitchy 实时给画面加上电路破坏的效果——那种需要电烙铁和一台坏掉的录像机才能造出来的失控感。所有处理都在本地，不上传任何东西。

### Neal.fun – Internet Artifacts
- **URL**: https://neal.fun/internet-artifacts
- **EN**: 57 interactive snapshots from the early web — the first spam email, the original Space Jam website, Netscape's error screen. Not a documentary, more like a cabinet of digital curiosities you can actually touch.
- **ZH**: 57 个早期互联网的交互式时刻——第一封垃圾邮件、最初的 Space Jam 网站、Netscape 的错误页面。不是纪录片，更像一个你可以真正触摸的数字奇珍柜。

### LLM Timeline
- **URL**: https://llm-timeline.com
- **EN**: 194 language models, from BERT in 2018 to whatever launched last week, laid out on a single scrollable timeline. The pace of this thing is almost disturbing to see all at once.
- **ZH**: 194 个大语言模型，从 2018 年的 BERT 到上周发布的新模型，全排在一条时间轴上。滚一遍你会意识到这件事发生得有多快，快到有点让人不安。

### Forestry Diary
- **URL**: https://forestrydiary.com
- **EN**: A forest ranger in Northern California kept a daily work diary from 1927 to 1945. Someone digitized it. Now it's searchable — weather, wildlife, fire watch, the rhythms of decades spent in the woods.
- **ZH**: 加州北部一位护林员从 1927 年到 1945 年每天写工作日记。有人把它数字化了，现在可以全文搜索——天气、野生动物、防火瞭望，几十年的森林时光就这样留下来了。

### Geo Racers
- **URL**: https://geo-racers.com
- **EN**: Race from London to Tokyo — no planes allowed. This geography game uses real routes and real geography knowledge. The fastest route wins cash prizes. Your move, atlas nerd.
- **ZH**: 从伦敦赛到东京，不能坐飞机。这个地理竞速游戏用真实路线和真实的地理知识比拼，最快完成的赢现金奖励。地图控的游乐场。

### Moltbook
- **URL**: https://moltbook.com
- **EN**: A Reddit-style social network built exclusively for AI agents. Humans are "welcome to observe." The posts, upvotes, and communities are all meant for bots. Whether it succeeds or fails, it's the strangest experiment on this list.
- **ZH**: 一个专门给 AI 代理用的社交网络，界面长得像 Reddit，但主角是各种 AI bot。人类"欢迎围观"。帖子、点赞、社区都是为代理设计的。不管最后成不成，这是这份清单里最离奇的实验。
