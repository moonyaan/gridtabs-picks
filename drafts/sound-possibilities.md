# Six Things You Can Do With Sound Now

From listening to the world to building your own — six tools that cover what's possible with sound today.

# 声音的六种可能

从听见世界到造出声音——当下声音创作的六个方向。

## Sites

### Radio.garden
- **URL**: https://radio.garden
- **EN**: Spin a 3D globe to tune into live radio from anywhere. Tokyo at 3am, a cafe in Paris, a truck stop in Nevada — thousands of stations, zero signup.
- **ZH**: 转动地球仪，听世界任何角落的电台。凌晨三点的东京、巴黎咖啡馆、内华达州卡车休息站——上千个电台，零门槛。

### Moodist
- **URL**: https://moodist.mvze.net
- **EN**: Layer 84 ambient sounds however you want. Rain + distant train + city hum. Free, open source, no account needed — just sound.
- **ZH**: 84 种环境音自由叠加。雨声 + 远处火车 + 城市低鸣。免费、开源、免注册——只有声音本身。

### Wispr Flow
- **URL**: https://wisprflow.ai
- **EN**: Talk instead of type. Your voice becomes structured writing in any app — not transcription, actual writing at 4x speed.
- **ZH**: 用说的代替打字。你的声音直接变成文章——不是逐字转录，是真的在写作，四倍速。

### Mubert
- **URL**: https://mubert.com
- **EN**: Need background music? Describe the mood, get a track that didn't exist five seconds ago. AI meets hundreds of musicians' stems — royalty-free.
- **ZH**: 视频需要配乐？描述氛围，拿到一段五秒前还不存在的音乐。AI 混合数百位音乐人的片段素材，免版权。

### ElevenLabs
- **URL**: https://elevenlabs.io
- **EN**: Text becomes voice — natural, expressive, multilingual. Clone your own or use theirs. Audiobooks, videos, apps all sound human now.
- **ZH**: 文字变成声音——自然、有情感、多语言。克隆你的声音，或直接用现成的。有声书、视频、App 听起来都像真人在说话。

### LALAL.AI
- **URL**: https://www.lalal.ai
- **EN**: Pull apart any audio. Isolate vocals, strip noise, separate instruments. What used to need a studio now happens in your browser.
- **ZH**: 拆解任何音频。分离人声、去除噪音、提取乐器。以前需要录音棚的事，现在浏览器里就能做。
