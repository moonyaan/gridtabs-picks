# The Focused Life: 9 Tools That Protect Your Attention

Distraction is the default. These tools push back — quietly, without drama. From ambient soundscapes to accountability sessions to writing spaces that hide the interface while you type.

# 专注就是一种选择：9 个保护注意力的工具

分心是默认状态，专注需要主动争取。这 9 个工具各有一套对抗干扰的哲学——有的用声音围住你，有的让界面消失，有的找个陌生人陪你坐着把事情做完。

## Sites

### Focusmate
- **URL**: https://focusmate.com
- **EN**: You show up, a stranger shows up, you both work for 25/50/75 minutes on camera — no chat, no pressure. Turns out just knowing someone else is sitting there is enough to stop you from opening another tab.
- **ZH**: 你打开摄像头，一个陌生人也打开，然后你们各自工作 25/50/75 分钟。不聊天，不打扰。仅仅是「有人在旁边」这件事，就能让你不再刷下一个标签页。

### Lofi.co
- **URL**: https://lofi.co
- **EN**: Lofi music layered with immersive pixel scenes — rainy café, night studio, quiet library. It's not just background noise; it's a small world you step into when you need to get something done.
- **ZH**: Lofi 音乐配上动态像素场景：雨天咖啡厅、深夜录音室、安静的图书馆。不只是背景音，更像是一个你切进去就能专注的小世界。

### Earth.fm
- **URL**: https://earth.fm
- **EN**: Field recordings from actual places on Earth — Borneo rainforest, Norwegian fjord, Namibian savanna. Unlike white noise apps, every soundscape here is a real location, and that specificity changes how it feels.
- **ZH**: 来自地球真实地点的声景录音——婆罗洲雨林、挪威峡湾、纳米比亚草原。不是合成的白噪音，是某个真实存在的地方此刻的声音，这个区别让人平静的方式完全不同。

### Calmly Writer
- **URL**: https://www.calmlywriter.com
- **EN**: When you're in focus mode, everything except the current paragraph fades away. The toolbar hides while you type. It's not a clever trick — it's actually the only way some things get written.
- **ZH**: 进入专注模式后，当前段落以外的一切都会变暗，工具栏在你打字时自动消失。这不是噱头——对有些人来说，这是唯一能让他们把东西写出来的方式。

### Sigilla
- **URL**: https://sigilla.net
- **EN**: A read-later app that auto-archives articles you never open. It quietly removes the clutter so your list stays short enough to actually use. Less a productivity tool, more a therapist for your digital hoarding habit.
- **ZH**: 一款会自动归档你从未打开的文章的稍后阅读工具。它悄悄清理掉积压的内容，让收藏列表短到你真的会去翻。与其说是效率工具，不如说是数字囤积习惯的解毒剂。

### DopaLoop
- **URL**: https://dopaloop.app
- **EN**: Habit tracking designed around how ADHD brains actually work — no punishing streaks, no shame when you miss a day, every habit tied to a bigger goal you care about. All data stays on your device.
- **ZH**: 按 ADHD 大脑的实际运作方式设计的习惯追踪器：断链不清零，不因漏打卡惩罚你，每个习惯都和你真正在乎的目标绑定。所有数据只存在你的设备上。

### Capacities
- **URL**: https://capacities.io
- **EN**: Notes organized around objects — a book, a person, a project — with links between them rather than folders on top of folders. It's designed to be a calm place to think, and it feels like one.
- **ZH**: 笔记以「对象」为单位组织——一本书、一个人、一个项目——彼此关联而非层层嵌套的文件夹。它把自己定位为一个平静的思考空间，用起来确实像是。

### Notchable
- **URL**: https://notchable.com
- **EN**: Lives in the MacBook notch, always a click away, never in the way. Voice-capture a thought in seconds, built-in Pomodoro timer, AI sorts the chaos. Built by people with ADHD who got tired of losing ideas to friction.
- **ZH**: 住在 MacBook 刘海里，随时可用，永远不挡路。几秒语音捕捉一个想法，内置番茄钟，AI 帮你整理乱局。由 ADHD 用户做给自己用的工具——受够了因为摩擦丢掉想法。

### Super Productivity
- **URL**: https://super-productivity.com
- **EN**: Open-source task manager with built-in time tracking and a dedicated deep work mode. Fully offline, no subscription, no vendor lock-in. The kind of tool you configure once and trust for years.
- **ZH**: 开源任务管理器，内置时间追踪和深度工作模式。完全离线，无订阅，数据只属于你。那种配置一次就可以用几年的工具。
