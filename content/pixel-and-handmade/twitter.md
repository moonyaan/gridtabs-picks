# Twitter Post: pixel-and-handmade

## Version A — 清单体

8 websites that still feel handmade:

1. Beats — pixel-art drum machine in your browser (beats.lasagna.pizza)
2. StoryMotion — turns diagrams into wobbly hand-drawn animations
3. Inklings — kids draw, parents write, it prints into a real book every month
4. Its Hover — an icon library where every single icon is animated by hand
5. Calmly Writer — focus mode dims everything except what you're writing
6. Remainders — your life as 4,160 dots, one per week, as phone wallpaper
7. Colorify Rocks — type "sunday morning" and get a color palette
8. Radio Garden — spin a 3D globe, land on any radio station on Earth

No algorithms. No AI-generated anything. Just people making things with care.
