# Sound Travel Without Algorithms

Eight places to stream music and ambient sound online — no algorithm, no recommendations, no curated playlists. Just you and whatever's playing.

# 算法消失之后，你在听什么

八个声音目的地，没有推荐、没有算法、没有人告诉你该听什么。你自己转动地球仪，选一个地方，听听那里的声音。

## Sites

### Radiooooo
- **URL**: https://radiooooo.com
- **EN**: A jukebox that spans every country and every decade since 1900. Pick a spot on the map, pick an era, and hear what was actually playing there — not a curated playlist, but a sonic time machine.
- **ZH**: 在世界地图上选一个国家，再拨一个年代——1970 年代的巴西、1950 年代的日本都有。它播放的不是歌单，是那个地方那个时代真实在播的音乐。

### Yamanote.fun
- **URL**: https://yamanote.fun
- **EN**: A 30-minute browser ride around Tokyo's Yamanote Line — platform jingles, arrival chimes, door sounds and all. The closest thing to a Tokyo commute you can have without a ticket.
- **ZH**: 30 分钟坐完东京山手线全程。30 个站的站台旋律、广播报站声、车门开合声一一收录。坐在电脑前，那种在地铁上发呆的感觉竟然真的来了。

### Conserve the Sound
- **URL**: https://www.conservethesound.de
- **EN**: An archive of disappearing everyday sounds — cassette fast-forward clicks, rotary dial phones, typewriter keys. These sounds defined whole eras and are quietly vanishing.
- **ZH**: 快消失的日常声音档案馆：卡带快进的吱呀声、老式拨号电话的转盘声、打字机的按键声。你以为会一直在的声音，正在悄悄消失。

### Poolsuite
- **URL**: https://poolsuite.net
- **EN**: A radio station built around the feeling of an endless summer. Seven channels, a vintage Mac OS interface, zero algorithm. Hit play, stay there — time passes differently.
- **ZH**: 永恒夏日电台，复古 Mac 界面，7 个频道，没有推荐没有跳过按钮。点击播放，就待在那个夏天里。

### Moodist
- **URL**: https://moodist.mvze.net
- **EN**: 84 ambient sounds you can layer however you like — rain, forest, café, brown noise. Free, open source, runs right in the browser. The difference from A Soft Murmur: more options, more granular sliders, and a community of custom presets to start from.
- **ZH**: 84 种环境音自由叠加，雨声、森林声、咖啡馆、色噪音，还有社区做好的预设可以直接用。比同类工具多出一层可玩性：颗粒度够细，调出来的声音是真正属于你的。

### Earth.fm
- **URL**: https://earth.fm
- **EN**: Real field recordings from rainforests, arctic tundra, coral reefs, mountain valleys — geolocated and immersive. Not white noise; actual places, with all their texture.
- **ZH**: 来自热带雨林、北极冻原、珊瑚礁、山谷溪流的真实野外录音。不是白噪音，是有地理坐标的真实场景，城市里的自然连接入口。

### A Soft Murmur
- **URL**: https://asoftmurmur.com
- **EN**: Rain, thunder, waves, fire, a coffee shop — blend them however you like. The simplest possible interface for people who just want to dial in a background and work.
- **ZH**: 雨声、雷声、海浪、篝火、咖啡馆，随意调节比例混合。界面极简，没有别的功能，就是让你找到属于自己的背景声然后工作。

### Chiptune Radio
- **URL**: https://chiptune-radio.alephvoid.com/
- **EN**: A non-stop stream of 8-bit game music — the sounds of Super Nintendo, Game Boy, and Commodore 64 eras, playing without interruption. Oddly focused.
- **ZH**: 8-bit 游戏音乐不间断直播——超级任天堂、Game Boy、Commodore 64 那个时代的声音。出乎意料地能让人专注。
