# 8 Absurd Websites That Prove the Internet Was Always Weird

The web was never supposed to be purely useful. These eight sites were built to waste your time in the most earnest, committed, delightful ways possible. No monetization strategy. No user journey. Just someone who had an idea and shipped it.

# 互联网一直都有点荒诞：8 个认真在开玩笑的网站

互联网从来不只是拿来用的。这 8 个网站的存在毫无实用价值——但每一个背后都有一个人真诚地、认真地做了一件荒唐事，然后把它扔上了网。打开一个，你就能感受到互联网最纯粹的那一面。

## Sites

### Zombo.com
- **URL**: https://zombo.com
- **EN**: Since 1999, Zombo.com has done exactly one thing: play a looping voice that says "Welcome to Zombo.com. You can do anything here." Nothing happens. Nothing is supposed to happen. It is a solemn declaration of infinite possibility backed by absolutely nothing. It is still online.
- **ZH**: 自 1999 年起，Zombo.com 只做一件事：循环播放一段合成语音，庄严地告诉你"欢迎来到 Zombo.com，在这里你能做任何事"。然后什么都不发生。这就是全部了。它就这样活到了 2026 年。

### HackerTyper
- **URL**: https://hackertyper.net
- **EN**: Press any key. Code scrolls across the screen. It doesn't matter what you type — the output is always the same impressive-looking source code. Hollywood hacking, simulated. Built for one moment: someone looks over your shoulder, and you look like a genius.
- **ZH**: 随便敲键盘，屏幕上就开始滚代码。打什么不重要，输出永远是那份看起来很厉害的源代码。专为一个瞬间而生：有人从你背后走过，你瞬间变成好莱坞黑客天才。

### Passweird
- **URL**: https://www.passweird.com
- **EN**: Generates passwords that are technically strong and practically unforgettable. They're also deeply strange. Examples include things like "correct-horse-battery-staple" but weirder and funnier. Security through absurdity.
- **ZH**: 生成技术上安全、实际上令人难忘的密码。它们也非常奇怪。每次刷新都是一个新的荒诞组合，你不会忘记，因为你无法相信自己正在用这个密码。通过荒诞实现安全。

### Find the Invisible Cow
- **URL**: https://findtheinvisiblecow.com
- **EN**: Move your mouse around the screen. The site yells "COW" — quietly when you're far away, loudly when you're close. That's it. That's the entire experience. The cow is invisible. You will find it anyway.
- **ZH**: 移动鼠标，网站会喊"COW"——你远的时候轻声喊，你近的时候大声喊。就这样。奶牛是隐形的。但你一定会找到它。而且你会觉得很有成就感。

### Scream Into the Void
- **URL**: https://screamintothevoid.com/
- **EN**: Type whatever you want to scream. The site screams it into the void. The text disappears forever, leaving no record. It's technically a mental health tool. It's also one of the most dramatically committed websites ever made.
- **ZH**: 输入你想呐喊的任何内容。网站帮你对虚空大声呐喊。然后文字永久消失，没有任何记录。从技术上说这是一个心理健康工具。但从审美上说，它是互联网上最戏剧化的网站之一。

### Is It a Pint?
- **URL**: https://isitapint.com
- **EN**: A grassroots citizen investigation into whether bars actually give you a full pint. Volunteers measure their drinks. Data is submitted. Statistics are compiled. It is the most rigorous research project ever conducted about a problem that should not need researching.
- **ZH**: 一场草根公民调查：酒吧有没有真的给你倒满一品脱？志愿者量自己的酒，提交数据，网站汇总统计。这是迄今为止最严肃的关于"到底有没有倒满"这个问题的研究项目。

### Is Hormuz Open Yet?
- **URL**: https://www.ishormuzopenyet.com
- **EN**: One question. One answer. The Strait of Hormuz — through which 20% of the world's oil supply passes — is either open or it isn't. This website tells you which. Every geopolitical crisis distilled to a yes or no. Updated in real time.
- **ZH**: 一个问题，一个回答。全球约 20% 的石油供应穿越霍尔木兹海峡——海峡要么开着，要么没开。这个网站实时告诉你答案。所有地缘政治复杂性，压缩成一个字：是/否。

### NeonFlames
- **URL**: https://neonflames.com
- **EN**: Move your mouse across a black canvas. Neon fire trails behind it. No saving. No purpose. Just light and motion in a browser window. Sometimes the internet is just for making something beautiful for ten minutes and then closing the tab.
- **ZH**: 在黑色画布上移动鼠标，霓虹火焰在你的轨迹后燃烧。没法保存，没有目的。只是在浏览器里做了十分钟美丽的事，然后关掉标签页。有时候，上网就是这样。
