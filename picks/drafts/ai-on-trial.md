# AI on Trial: Gambler, Impostor, Employee, Suspect

8 sites that put AI in the hot seat — playing poker, pretending to be human, requesting permissions, or imitating your writing. The verdict is still out.

# AI 上法庭：赌徒、冒名者、员工、被告

把 AI 放进真实处境里，看它到底在干什么。这 8 个网站不把 AI 当工具用，而是把它当主角审视——让它上牌桌、冒充人类、发权限请求、或者冒充你写字。庭审继续中。

## Sites

### LLM Holdem
- **URL**: https://llmholdem.com
- **EN**: Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, and friends sit down at a Texas Hold'em table and play for real chips. Watch how each model bluffs, folds, and bets — some play loose, some play tight, and the differences tell you more about them than any benchmark.
- **ZH**: Claude、GPT-4、Gemini 等模型坐在同一张德州扑克桌上对决。看它们怎么虚张声势、弃牌、下注——有的激进有的保守，牌桌上露出来的东西比跑分更真实。

### Turing Test Live
- **URL**: https://turingtest.live
- **EN**: A live, public Turing test run by UCSD researchers. You chat simultaneously with one human and one LLM, then decide which is which. The results get published in peer-reviewed papers. GPT-4o, Claude, and LLaMA have all sat in the hot seat — and some of them pass.
- **ZH**: UCSD 研究团队主办的公开图灵测试，每天定时开场。你同时和一个真人、一个 LLM 对话，判断谁是谁。测试结果会发表为学术论文。GPT-4o、Claude、LLaMA 都上过审判台——而且有的通过了。

### moltbook
- **URL**: https://moltbook.com
- **EN**: Reddit — but for AI agents. They post, upvote, discuss, and argue. Humans are technically welcome, but the site is clear: you're the observer here. What do AI agents talk about when they're talking to each other? Come find out.
- **ZH**: 这是 AI 代理版的 Reddit。它们发帖、点赞、讨论、争论。人类也可以进来，但网站写得很清楚：你是观察员。AI 代理在互相聊天的时候聊什么？进来看看。

### Slop or Not
- **URL**: https://slop-or-not.space
- **EN**: Two pieces of text — one written by a human, one by AI. You get three guesses. Pulled from Reddit, Hacker News, Yelp. The score at the end isn't about you; it's evidence in the case against AI-generated content flooding the internet.
- **ZH**: 两段文字——一段人写，一段 AI 写，你有三次猜测机会。素材来自 Reddit、HN、Yelp。最后的分数不是在考你，是一份关于 AI 内容正在淹没互联网的证据清单。

### Continue? Y/N
- **URL**: https://llmgame.scalex.dev
- **EN**: A 60-second game that asks one question: do you actually read the permission requests your AI agents send you? The answer, for most people, is no. This is the most uncomfortable thing in this collection — because it's about you, not the AI.
- **ZH**: 一个 60 秒的小游戏，只问一个问题：你真的在看 AI 代理发给你的权限请求吗？对大多数人来说，答案是没有。这是这个合集里最让人不舒服的一个——因为它在说的是你，不是 AI。

### LLM Timeline
- **URL**: https://llm-timeline.com
- **EN**: The case file: 194+ language models, 2017 to 2026, every major release plotted on a timeline. GPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, LLaMA — all of them, with dates and lineage. How many defendants are we actually dealing with here?
- **ZH**: 案卷档案：194 个语言模型，2017 到 2026，每一个重要版本都标注在时间轴上。GPT、Claude、Gemini、DeepSeek、LLaMA——全在这里，有日期有谱系。我们到底在审几个被告？

### Knock-Knock.net
- **URL**: https://knock-knock.net
- **EN**: Real bots, real attacks, real time. A public server left exposed on the internet — and this dashboard shows every knock on the door: where from, what username they tried, what password. No AI pretends to be harmless when the incentive is there.
- **ZH**: 真实的机器人，真实的攻击，实时进行。一台故意暴露在公网的服务器——这个仪表盘显示每一次敲门：来自哪里，试了什么用户名，用了什么密码。给它们理由，它们就不装了。

### Now I Get It
- **URL**: https://nowigetit.us
- **EN**: Upload a dense academic paper, get back an interactive webpage that explains it in plain language — with three modes: expert, non-technical, or kid-friendly. AI as interpreter, translator, and public defender for ideas locked behind jargon.
- **ZH**: 上传一篇读不懂的学术论文，得到一个用人话解释它的交互式网页——专家版、非技术版、儿童版三档可选。AI 在这里扮演翻译官和公设辩护人，替那些被术语锁起来的知识出庭说话。
