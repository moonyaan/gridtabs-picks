# The Internet Used to Feel Like This: 8 Sites That Still Do

Open one of these and you're back in a dorm room, 2002, dial-up humming. The browser was a portal to something genuinely weird and handmade. These eight sites never got the memo that the internet grew up.

# 浏览器记忆：8 个让时光倒流的网站

那时候上网，是真的会有「哇」的感觉。这 8 个网站，有的复活了拨号时代，有的把 YouTube 变成深夜有线电视，有的让你在 Excel 里打扫雷——它们活在 2026 年，心却留在 2002 年。

## Sites

### Windows 93
- **URL**: https://windows93.net/
- **EN**: A fully functional fake OS running in your browser — complete with a desktop, apps, games, and easter eggs you'll spend an hour finding. It's the most ambitious browser art project you've never opened.
- **ZH**: 一个完全可用的假操作系统，直接在浏览器里跑。有桌面、有应用、有游戏、有彩蛋，还有让人不明觉厉的 "Windows 93" 感。打开以后不要说我没提醒你——你会在里面待很久。

### GifCities
- **URL**: https://gifcities.org/
- **EN**: A searchable archive of 1.6 million animated GIFs salvaged from GeoCities before Yahoo pulled the plug. Type anything — "cat," "under construction," "dancing baby" — and fall through the floor of early internet history.
- **ZH**: GeoCities 在 2009 年关站前，有人把 160 万个动态 GIF 全部存了下来。现在你可以搜索它们。搜 "cat"，搜 "welcome"，搜 "under construction"——每一条结果都是一个 1997 年的个人主页碎片。

### WebAIM — AIM in the Browser
- **URL**: https://www.webaim.xyz
- **EN**: A working recreation of AOL Instant Messenger, running entirely in a browser with the original Windows 98 interface, buddy lists, away messages, and that unmistakable door-opening sound. It's not a screenshot — it works.
- **ZH**: 完全复刻的 AOL Instant Messenger，Windows 98 界面，真实可用的好友列表、离开状态、聊天记录。最重要的是那个开门音效还在。这不是截图，是真的在浏览器里跑起来了。

### Channel Surfer
- **URL**: https://channelsurfer.tv
- **EN**: Turns your YouTube subscriptions into a cable TV program guide — complete with a grid schedule and a remote control. Click a show, it plays. Change the channel, something else comes on. You forgot how good random TV felt.
- **ZH**: 把你的 YouTube 订阅变成复古有线电视节目表。有格状排期，有遥控器，点一个节目它就播，切频道另一个开始。你忘了随机换台是什么感觉——这个网站会帮你想起来。

### Sure.is ANSI Viewer
- **URL**: https://sure.is/ansi/
- **EN**: Before JPEGs, before GIFs, before the web — there was ANSI art. Colored text characters arranged into images, spread through bulletin board systems at 2400 baud. This viewer lets you watch them play back the way they were meant to: slow, line by line, with a blinking cursor.
- **ZH**: 比 GIF 更早，比网页更早——那个年代的"图片"是用彩色文字字符拼起来的 ANSI 艺术，通过拨号 BBS 传播。这个网站让你按当年的速度重放它们：一行一行，带着闪烁的光标。

### Springfield Oracle
- **URL**: https://www.springfieldoracle.com/
- **EN**: The Simpsons has "predicted" an implausible number of real events. This database catalogs every one — with the original episode, the real-world event, and whether it actually checks out. More rigorous than it has any right to be.
- **ZH**: 辛普森一家到底"预言"了多少真实事件？这个数据库把每一条都收录了——原版集数、对应的真实事件、以及是否真的算数。比你预期的严肃认真太多。

### Poolsuite
- **URL**: https://poolsuite.net
- **EN**: A music station designed to feel like perpetual summer, dressed in a Mac OS 9 interface from 1999. Seven channels, no algorithm, no skipping. You pick a vibe and let it play. It's not streaming — it's atmosphere.
- **ZH**: 永恒夏日音乐电台，穿着 1999 年 Mac OS 9 的皮。7 个频道，没有算法，不能随意跳过。你选一个氛围，然后让它播。这不是串流，是沉浸。

### Bored Spreadsheet
- **URL**: https://boredspreadsheet.com/workbook
- **EN**: Classic games — Minesweeper, 2048, Solitaire, Sudoku — dressed up to look exactly like a corporate Excel workbook. Every meeting, every open-office shift, every boring afternoon now has a cover story.
- **ZH**: 扫雷、2048、纸牌、数独——全部伪装成 Excel 工作簿的样子。每次会议、每个开放式办公室的下午、每个无聊的摸鱼时光，现在都有了完美的掩护。
