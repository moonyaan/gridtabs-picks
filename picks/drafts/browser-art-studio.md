# Make Art in Your Browser: 8 Free Creative Tools

Pixel art, generative graphics, audio editing, glitch camera — 8 browser tools for making things. No install, no account, just open and create.

# 8个浏览器创作工具，打开即用，无需安装

打开标签页，就是打开了一间工作室。这 8 个网站把浏览器变成了画布、暗室、调音台和绘图机——不用安装，不用注册，打开就能开始。

## Sites

### Zazow
- **URL**: https://zazow.com
- **EN**: Dial parameters and watch the algorithm paint — fractals, plasma fields, Bauhaus shapes, Spiro curves. Unique every time. No code needed.
- **ZH**: 调节参数，让算法替你作画——分形、等离子场、包豪斯几何、螺线图。每次生成都是独一无二的，可以直接下载。不用写任何代码。

### Immersive Garden
- **URL**: https://immersive-g.com
- **EN**: Paris studio building WebGL experiences for luxury brands. Their own site is the portfolio — scroll it like an art installation. Proof of what browsers can render.
- **ZH**: 巴黎创意工作室，专为奢侈品牌打造沉浸式 WebGL 体验。他们的官网本身就是作品集——滚动浏览就像走进一个装置艺术展。看看浏览器能渲染出什么。

### Piskel
- **URL**: https://www.piskelapp.com
- **EN**: Browser pixel art and sprite animation editor. Draw frame by frame, preview live, export as GIF or spritesheet. Classic retro game art tool, no file manager needed.
- **ZH**: 免费的浏览器像素艺术和精灵动画编辑器。逐帧绘制，实时预览动画，导出 GIF 或精灵表。做复古游戏美术的经典工具，从画稿到动画全在标签页里完成。

### Turtletoy
- **URL**: https://www.turtletoy.net
- **EN**: Write JavaScript turtle commands, get SVG/GCODE for a pen plotter or laser cutter. All community creations are open-source and remixable. Generative art meets physical craft.
- **ZH**: 写 JavaScript 海龟指令，生成适配绘图机和激光切割机的 SVG/GCODE 文件。所有社区作品开源可 remix。写代码，最终拿到一张纸上真实的线条。

### Patatap
- **URL**: https://patatap.com
- **EN**: Hit any key — a shape blooms on screen, a sound fires. Each key is a unique animation-sound pair. A musical instrument made of keystrokes. Five minutes here never feel wasted.
- **ZH**: 按下任意键，屏幕上绽放一个形状，同时触发一段音效。每个键是不同的动画+音效组合。这是一件完全由按键演奏的乐器——在这里消磨五分钟完全不觉得浪费。

### AudioMass
- **URL**: https://audiomass.co
- **EN**: Multi-track audio editor, entirely in the browser. Trim, loop, mix, apply filters — no upload, no account, no install. Makes you wonder why you ever paid for desktop software.
- **ZH**: 完全在浏览器内运行的多轨音频编辑器。剪辑、循环、叠加音轨、添加滤镜——无需上传文件，无需账号，无需安装。用完之后会疑惑：当年为什么要花钱买桌面软件。

### Palette Inspiration
- **URL**: https://paletteinspiration.com
- **EN**: 23,000 palettes from 23,000 masterworks, 3,000 artists. Browse by style, genre, or artist. Export to CSS, Tailwind, Figma JSON, or ASE. Art history as a design tool.
- **ZH**: 2.3 万幅大师画作，2.3 万套配色方案，3000 位艺术家。按风格、流派、艺术家浏览，可导出为 CSS、Tailwind、Figma JSON 或 ASE 格式。把艺术史变成设计工具。

### Glitchy
- **URL**: https://glitchycam.com
- **EN**: Open your camera and watch real-time glitch effects distort the feed live. Color channels split, pixels scatter — all local, nothing uploaded. Instant glitch art, zero setup.
- **ZH**: 在浏览器里打开摄像头，实时看到电路破坏风格的 glitch 效果扭曲画面。色彩通道分裂、图像损坏、像素散射——全部本地处理，不上传任何内容。即开即用的 glitch 艺术，零配置。
