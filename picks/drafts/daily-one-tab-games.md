# Daily One-Tab Games: 6 Browser Challenges, One Per Day

6 browser games built around a single daily challenge — GeoGuessr, putt.day, Gerrymandle, and more. No download, no account, one tab is all you need.

# 每日一关：6 个打开就能玩的浏览器挑战

每天一个挑战，打开标签页就开始，不用下载，不用注册。这 6 个浏览器游戏，每个都围绕一道每日谜题设计。

## Sites

### putt.day
- **URL**: https://putt.day
- **EN**: One hole, one swing, one chance — then it's done for the day. Drag to aim, compare your score with friends, and close the tab. The daily game ritual distilled to its absolute minimum.
- **ZH**: 每天一个球洞，瞄准、挥杆、收工。它把每日打卡做到了最小可能的单位——一次机会，然后去做今天其他的事。

### GeoGuessr
- **URL**: https://www.geoguessr.com
- **EN**: Dropped into a random Street View somewhere on Earth — no hints, just the road and the sky. The daily challenge gives everyone the same location, turning geography into a shared daily riddle.
- **ZH**: 随机落进地球某个角落的街景，靠路牌、植被、建筑风格猜你在哪。每日挑战模式让全球玩家同时破解同一道地理谜题。

### The Wiki Game
- **URL**: https://www.thewikigame.com
- **EN**: Start at one Wikipedia article, reach the target using only hyperlinks, in as few clicks as possible. A game about how human knowledge is connected — and how to get from "Spaghetti" to "Cold War" in four jumps.
- **ZH**: 从一篇维基百科出发，只能点超链接，用最少步骤抵达目标词条。玩几局会发现：人类知识的网络比你想象的更紧密，也更迂回。

### Gerrymandle
- **URL**: https://gerrymandle.cc
- **EN**: Every day, a new hexagonal map — redraw district lines so your party wins more seats. The lesson lands fast: this is exactly how real electoral maps get manipulated, in ten minutes.
- **ZH**: 在六边形网格上重划选区，让你的政党赢得更多席位。每天一道新题，十分钟学懂选区操控——比任何政治课都直观。

### BrainFrog
- **URL**: https://brainfrog.lol
- **EN**: One lateral-thinking puzzle per day. Not trivia — questions designed to break your default reasoning so you arrive at answers from an unexpected direction. Small dose, significant effect.
- **ZH**: 每天一道需要跳出常规的益智题。不是考知识，是考你能不能在第一直觉失效之后找到另一条路。做完一题，大脑刚好活动开了。

### Zanagrams
- **URL**: https://zanagrams.com
- **EN**: Scrambled letters, a timer, the challenge of assembling as many valid words as possible. Vocabulary helps, but the real edge is seeing arrangements that others miss.
- **ZH**: 给你一堆打乱的字母，看你能拼出多少个合法单词。词汇量是优势，但真正拉开差距的是你能看见别人看不见的那些排列。
