# Inbox Zero for Everything: 9 Tools to Clear the Backlog

Not a productivity category — a feeling: the specific relief of having one place for all your messages, one memory for your clipboard, one AI that actually knows your patterns, so your brain can finally stop holding everything at once.

# 全面清零：从收件箱到剪贴板，夺回你的注意力

你的大脑不应该是便利贴墙。这 9 个工具分别替你管住一种"积压"——消息乱、历史记录消失、会议内容忘光、每次都要重新解释自己——把"我得记着这件事"从你的头脑里一条条清出去。

## Sites

### Franz
- **URL**: https://meetfranz.com
- **EN**: Every messaging app you juggle — WhatsApp, Slack, Gmail, Signal, Discord — lives in one window, one sidebar. The AI summarizes unread threads across platforms before you even open them. The mental overhead of "which app was that in" disappears.
- **ZH**: 75 个消息平台聚合进一个窗口，Slack、WhatsApp、Gmail 都在左侧列表里，AI 在你点开前就把未读消息摘要好。再也不用想"那条消息是在哪个 App 里"。

### Paste
- **URL**: https://pasteapp.io
- **EN**: Everything you've ever copied is still there, searchable, pasteable in any order. The stuff you copy ten times a day because you can't find the original? It was in Paste the whole time. Privacy rules let it auto-skip passwords and card numbers.
- **ZH**: 你复制过的所有内容都留着，可以全文搜索，按任意顺序粘贴。那些你每天要重新复制五次的内容？它们一直都在 Paste 里，你只是没有一个地方找。

### Town
- **URL**: https://town.com
- **EN**: Town's AI studies your reply patterns and phrasing, then drafts responses in your voice before you open the thread. Not a chatbot you prompt — an assistant that's already done the thinking.
- **ZH**: Townie 先看你历史上怎么回邮件、跟谁说话用什么语气，然后在你打开收件箱之前就把草稿准备好。不是你给它出题，是它先替你想。

### Littlebird
- **URL**: https://littlebird.ai
- **EN**: Littlebird passively builds a searchable memory of your day across every app — docs, emails, meetings. Ask "what did I decide last Tuesday" and it actually knows.
- **ZH**: 后台追踪文档、邮件、会议，建立可搜索的工作记忆。"上周二那个会决定了什么"，它真的记得。

### Granola
- **URL**: https://www.granola.ai
- **EN**: No meeting bot joins your calls. Granola quietly captures audio from your Mac, then turns whatever scattered notes you jotted into a clean structured document after the call ends. Meeting backlog, cleared.
- **ZH**: 没有 Bot 加入你的会议。Granola 悄悄录下音频，会后把你随手记的碎片笔记整理成结构化文档。那一堆"会后要整理"，直接清掉。

### Typeahead
- **URL**: https://typeahead.ai
- **EN**: Wherever you type — email, Slack, Notion, code — Typeahead reads context and suggests the next phrase. Hit Tab. Entirely local, never touches a server.
- **ZH**: 在任何应用里打字，Typeahead 读懂上下文，按 Tab 接受建议。完全本地运行，不上传任何内容。

### Daigest
- **URL**: https://daige.st
- **EN**: Daigest monitors Reddit, RSS, YouTube, and Slack, then delivers one curated briefing on your schedule. You define what matters. It filters the rest.
- **ZH**: 与其每天逐个刷 Reddit、RSS、YouTube、Slack，不如让 Daigest 监控它们，按你设定的时间推送一份定制简报。你定义什么重要，它过滤其余的。信息消费从被动变成主动。

### Lindy
- **URL**: https://www.lindy.ai
- **EN**: Email triage, scheduling, follow-ups, CRM updates — set a Lindy workflow once and it runs 24/7. The administrative layer of your day, automated.
- **ZH**: 邮件分类、会议排期、跟进提醒、CRM 更新——Lindy 接管这些行政杂务，你只需要设置一次工作流，它就 24 小时自动跑。收件箱空下来，日历自己填满。

### SimpleLogin
- **URL**: https://simplelogin.io
- **EN**: Each site you sign up for gets its own alias. Spam hits the alias, not your inbox. A service leaks or annoys you — delete the alias. Gone. Your real inbox stays clean from the start.
- **ZH**: 不要再把真实邮箱给每个注册的网站。SimpleLogin 为每个服务生成一个独立别名，垃圾邮件打到别名上，不是你的真实收件箱。哪个服务烦了就删掉那个别名。收件箱从源头就干净。
